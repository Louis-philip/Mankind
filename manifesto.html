Manifesto

Mankind media believes that the study of science, as well as its application to solve practical and societal problems, resulting in technology, is not only an essential part of what it means to be human, but also something that needs to be shared and passed on from generation to generation, so that progress is healthy and constant. Here, our aim is to create a space conducive to sharing the most interesting science and technology topics of the moment, as well as perpetuating the great achievements of the past... All with a touch of artistic depth and good design, of course.
